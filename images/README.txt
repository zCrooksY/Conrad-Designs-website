CONRAD DESIGNS — PHOTOS FOLDER
==============================

Drop your photos in THIS folder using the exact filenames below.
The website automatically uses them as soon as they're here.
Until a file exists, the site shows a temporary placeholder instead.

USE THESE EXACT NAMES (all lowercase, .jpg):

  hero-kitchen.jpg   -> Big hero image, top of the page.
                        Best results: TALL (portrait) photo, e.g. 900 x 1125.
                        Use your single best kitchen or bathroom shot.

  work-1.jpg         -> Gallery: large landscape shot (wide kitchen)
  work-2.jpg         -> Gallery: tall portrait shot (shower / wet room)
  work-3.jpg         -> Gallery: tall portrait shot (bathroom)
  work-4.jpg         -> Gallery: wide landscape shot (kitchen)
  work-5.jpg         -> Gallery: landscape shot (en-suite / detail)
  work-6.jpg         -> Gallery: landscape shot (worktop / detail)

  before.jpg         -> "Before" photo for the drag-slider (landscape)
  after.jpg          -> "After" photo of the SAME room (landscape)
                        Tip: take both from the same spot for the best effect.

  conrad.jpg         -> Photo of you / on-site portrait (TALL/portrait, 800 x 1000)


TIPS
----
- JPG only, and keep the names exactly as above (no spaces, no capitals).
- If your phone saves photos as .HEIC, open and "Save as JPEG" first.
- Landscape = wider than tall.  Portrait = taller than wide.
- Aim for large, sharp photos (1000px+ wide). Good light makes a huge difference.
- You don't need all of them at once — add what you have; the rest stay as placeholders.

WANT DIFFERENT NAMES OR MORE PHOTOS?
------------------------------------
Just tell Claude the filenames you've got and it'll wire them in for you.
